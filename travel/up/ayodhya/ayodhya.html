Ayodhya,U.P

Ayodhya, the birthplace of Lord Rama, is a city of immense religious and historical significance. Situated on the banks of the Sarayu River, Ayodhya is a spiritual hub that attracts pilgrims and travelers from all over the world.

Historical and Cultural Significance

Known as one of the seven sacred cities of Hinduism, Ayodhya has a rich past that dates back to ancient times. The city is home to several temples, including the newly constructed Ram Mandir, which stands as a symbol of faith and devotion.

Places to Visit

  • Ram Janmabhoomi
  • Hanuman Garhi
  • Sarayu River Ghats

My Experience

Walking through the vibrant streets of Ayodhya, I felt an aura of spirituality and peace.
